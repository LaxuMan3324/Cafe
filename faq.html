Frequent Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our campus cafe

All Hours Menu Payment Events Other
What are the operating hours of CST Cafe?

CST Cafe is open Monday through Friday from 7:30 AM to 9:00 PM and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and major holidays.

During finals week, we extend our hours until 11:00 PM on weekdays to support your late-night study sessions!

What food and drink options are available?

We offer a wide variety of options including:

  • Specialty coffee drinks (espresso, lattes, cold brew)
  • Fresh smoothies and juices
  • Breakfast items (bagels, pastries, breakfast sandwiches)
  • Lunch options (sandwiches, wraps, salads, soups)
  • Grab-and-go snacks
  • Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options

Our menu rotates seasonally and we feature weekly specials created by culinary students!

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • Cash
  • All major credit/debit cards
  • Mobile payments (mBob,mPay,etc)
  • The CST Cafe app

Load money onto your app for a 5% discount on all purchases!

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes! We understand the importance of catering to various dietary needs. We offer:

  • Vegetarian and vegan options
  • Gluten-free items
  • Dairy-free alternatives for all drinks
  • Nut-free preparation areas
  • Detailed allergen information available upon request

Please inform our staff about any allergies or dietary restrictions so we can best accommodate your needs.

Can I host events or study groups at CST Cafe?

Absolutely! CST Cafe offers spaces for group activities:

  • Small groups (4-8 people) can use our open seating area
  • Larger groups can reserve our meeting room (capacity: 20 people)
  • Student clubs receive priority booking for official meetings
  • Special catering options available for events

To reserve space, please fill out the form on our website or speak with a manager at least 48 hours in advance.

Is there free Wi-Fi available?

Yes! CST Cafe provides complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi for all customers. Simply connect to the "CST-Cafe-Guest" network and accept the terms of service to get online.

Students and faculty can also access the secure campus network "CST-Secure" using their university credentials for enhanced security.

Are there student employment opportunities?

Yes! CST Cafe is largely staffed by students. We offer:

  • Flexible work schedules that accommodate class times
  • Competitive campus wages starting at Nu.215/hour
  • Free meals during shifts
  • Valuable work experience
  • Management opportunities for long-term employees

Applications are accepted year-round, with peak hiring periods at the beginning of each semester. Apply through the campus employment portal or in person at the cafe.

Do you host special events or promotions?

We love creating special experiences for our campus community! Regular events include:

  • Open Mic Nights - Every other Thursday from 7-9 PM
  • Finals Week Fuel - Extended hours and special discounts
  • Themed Food Weeks - Cultural cuisines featured monthly
  • Coffee Tastings - Learn about different roasts and brewing methods
  • Student Art Showcase - We display student artwork on our walls

Follow us on social media or check our events board for upcoming special events.
