About CST Cafe

Where Education Meets Great Coffee

Our Story

The Beginning of Something Special

CST Cafe was founded in 2015 from a simple idea: create a space where students and faculty could enjoy premium coffee in a comfortable environment that fosters creativity, collaboration, and community.

What started as a small kiosk with just two baristas has grown into the heart of campus life. Our commitment to quality beverages, healthy food options, and creating a welcoming atmosphere has made CST Cafe the favorite gathering spot for students and professors alike.

Over the years, we've expanded our menu, our space, and our impact on campus life, but our core mission remains the same: to serve great coffee and create meaningful connections.
